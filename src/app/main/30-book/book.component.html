Snorkelling Adventures in Britain

Snorkelling is the perfect family adventure for people who love the water and the natural world. It is free and accessible to people of all ages and abilities, but there is a skill in selecting safe sites with abundant underwater life.

We are working with Wild Things Publishing to release our first book - all about Snorkelling in Britain!

The book will be packed with information on the best sites to snorkel across Britain, complimented with inspiring photography, clear site finding advice and vital tips to get you started.
