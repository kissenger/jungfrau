What is Snorkelology?

Hello! We are Emma and Gordon, and we big fans of spending time outdoors particularly in, on and around the water. One of our favourite things to do individually and as a family is exploring the seas around the British coastline armed with a mask and snorkel (or even just a pair of goggles)...

We launched this site as a way to connect with others who want to find out more about British Snorkelling...

We had our first exposure to the wonders of British sea life through SCUBA diving, which we loved so much that we both became instructors. Then came the challenge of babies, and our water-based forays became more family focussed (sneaky swims on family beach days). It was these quick dips with nothing but a pair of goggles which reminded us that you don't need lots of kit to explore the beautiful underwater world. Increasingly we began to pack our mask and snorkel on our family trips, the kids joined in and we were reminded that a snorkelling is a fun, rewarding and extremely accessible activity that the whole family can enjoy!

Snorkelling Adventures in Britain

Snorkelling is the perfect family adventure for people who love the water and the natural world. It is free and accessible to people of all ages and abilities, but there is a skill in selecting safe sites with abundant underwater life.

We are working with Wild Things Publishing to release our first book - all about Snorkelling in Britain!

The book will be packed with information on the best sites to snorkel across Britain, complimented with inspiring photography, clear site finding advice and vital tips to get you started.
