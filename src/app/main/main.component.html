What is Snorkelology?

We are Emma and Gordon. When not working we spend as much time as possible outdoors - running, swimming, paddleboarding, cycling, climbing, skiing and ... planning our next adventures!

We launched this site as a way to connect with others who want to find out more about British Snorkelling...

Before our children were born we were enthusiastic SCUBA divers, enventually becoming diving instructors and later on moving into the world of techinical (deeper/mixed gas diving). After a hiatus of about 10 years spent holding babies, herding toddlers and working around the needs of the smaller-legged members of the family, we are now rediscovering the marine world through our family snorkelling adventures.

Snorkelling Adventures in Britain

Snorkelling is the perfect family adventure for people who love the water and the natural world . It is free and accessible to people of all ages and abilities, but there is a skill in selecting safe sites with abundant underwater life.

We are working with Wild Things Publishing to release our first book - all about Snorkelling in Britain!

The book will be packed with information on the best sites to snorkel across Britain, complimented with inspiring photography, clear site finding advice and vital tips to get you started. Follow our blog as we discover more about the challenges of writing your first book with two young children (and an excitable dog) in tow...

Our friends and partners...

Wild Things Publishing publish inspiring and beautiful guide books for explorers of all kinds.
Jen and Sim write award-winning guide-books complimented with beautiful photography, and are super lovely people to boot!
The Seal Alliance are dedicated to protecting seals and their habitats - we are working together to promote responsible interactions with these very special mammals.
The Scottish Wildlife Trust are also great believers in snorkelling as a fantasic way to explore our coastal waters.
The British Sub-Aqua Club (BSAC) offer training to suit all needs; a great place to start your snorkelling journey.
