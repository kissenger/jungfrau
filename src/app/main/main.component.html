What is Snorkelology?

Hello and welcome to Snorkelology - a website for anyone interested in British snorkelling.

We are Emma and Gordon, and we launched this site as a way to reach others who want to find out and share information about snorkelling in around our wonderful coastline. We are both keen outdoor enthusiasts who use our spare time running, swimming, cyling, climbing, skiing and ... planning our next adventures! Before we had children we were enthusiastic SCUBA divers, both achieving instuctor standard and enjoying undersea adventures together around the world.

With our children now rapidly approaching their teens we are keen to introduce them to the undersea world, and there is no better family adventure than British snorkelling! With the boom in open water (or 'wild') swimming we are keen to share the simple pleasure of taking that one step further - with the addition of some cheap and simple kit we can all share the rich and diverse natural marine environment awaiting us on our doorstep.

Snorkelling Adventures in Britain

We are excited to anounce that we have partnered with Wild Things Publishing to release our first book with the working title "Wild Swimming Marine Explorer: Best Snorkelling and Underwater Adventures in Britain"

The book will be packed with information on the best sites to snorkel across Britain, complimented with inspiring photography, clear site finding advice and vital tips to get you started. Follow our blog as we discover more about the challenges of writing your first book with two young children in tow...
